Complex Number defined

A complex number is an expression consisting of two parts; the real number part and the imaginary number part. It helps you express expressions that would be impossible to be expressed in basic maths. The expression is defined as follows; a+bi where a is the real number, b is the real number and ibeing an imaginary unit that satisfies the equation i2= −1

Complex Number Library

The Library created allows manipulation of the any two complex number. The manipulation functions include; addition, subtraction, multiplication and addition of complex numbers

Examples

var x = {8+9i}; where x.re; //8 x.im; //9

var y = {2+i}; where y.re; //2 y.im; //1

x.sum(x, y); gives 10+10i;
