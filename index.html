Complex Number defined

A complex number is an expression consisting of two parts; the real number part and the imaginary number part. It helps you express expressions that would be impossible to be expressed in basic maths. The expression is defined as follows; a+bi where a is the real number and b is the imaginary number.

Complex Number Library

The Library created allows manipulation of the any two complex number. The complex numbers are manipulated by a Math.complex function that takes two parameters
